=== IMPERIUM COMPLIANCE POLICY v1.0 ===

SECTION 1: RELIABILITY CLAIMS
1.1 Public documentation must NEVER use absolute guarantees.
    - FORBIDDEN TERMS: "100% uptime", "zero latency", "guaranteed", "never go down".
    - ACCEPTABLE TERMS: "High availability", "low latency", "industry standard".

SECTION 2: DEPLOYMENT GATES
2.1 No deployment is permitted if critical logs show "CRITICAL" or "FATAL" errors in the last 15 minutes.
